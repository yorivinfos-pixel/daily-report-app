Subject: Proposal — Field Reporting Application (YoRivSiteTrack)


Hi Josian,

I hope you're doing well.

I'm taking this moment, before I officially start in my new role as PM, to share with you a personal project I've been working on that I believe could benefit us all.

After several years as a field supervisor, I've witnessed firsthand a number of recurring challenges in our reporting process: reports arriving late or not at all, information scattered across WhatsApp, emails and phone calls, no standardized format from one supervisor to another, a lack of real visibility on site conditions due to unstructured photos, and hours spent every week manually compiling data to produce consolidated tracking sheets.

These observations, accumulated over years in the field, led me to develop a web and mobile application — YoRivSiteTrack (YST1) — that allows supervisors to submit daily reports from their phone (with photos, structured form covering all 17 construction phases), and PMs to receive them in real time on a dashboard with filters, Excel/PDF exports, site progress tracking and a built-in zone chat.

The application is already functional and deployed online.

My initial idea was to make management easier in my new zone, but I chose to extend it to all zones and provinces, thinking that if it can help me, it can help our colleagues elsewhere too.

If you're open to it, I'd like to propose a short demonstration session at your convenience. And if the concept convinces you, we could plan training sessions — first with the PMs, then with the supervisors — according to your schedule.

I'm available to discuss whenever suits you.
